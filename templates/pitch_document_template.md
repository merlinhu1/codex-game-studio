# Purpose

Summarize a game concept for audience, differentiation, feasibility, and production promise.

# Inputs

- Concept, target audience, references, competitors, platform, genre, team capacity, and risks.

# Outputs

- Elevator pitch, pillars, differentiators, visual/audio hook, production scope, and validation asks.

# Validation

- Pitch is specific, falsifiable, and clear about prototype vs full-production promises.
